Most frequently asked questions

What is Astrology?

Astrology, type of divination that involves the forecasting of earthly and human events through the observation and interpretation of the fixed stars, the Sun, the Moon, and the planets.

Is astrology real?

It is pretty hard to confirm and deny the plausibility of astrology being real. There are contradictory claims going about astrology and zodiacs. Some research done by the community conforms to the belief that astrology is real. On the other hand, there is data available to prove the notion wrong.

Does astrology affect life?

Scientific studies have not found evidence to support the claims of astrology. However, many people find personal meaning and guidance in astrology, and it can be a source of comfort and insight for some individuals. It's important to approach astrology with a critical mindset and to recognize that its impact on a person's life can vary greatly from individual to individual.

Where does astrology come from?

It originated in Mesopotamia (c. 3rd millennium BC) and spread to India, but it developed its Western form in Greek civilization during the Hellenistic period. Astrology entered Islamic culture as part of the Greek tradition and was returned to European culture through Arabic learning during the Middle Ages.

How can astrology help you?

Astrology supports the existence of free will and rejects superstition and fatalism. It also allows us a more objective understanding of our character, recognition of our talents and potentials, and an opportunity for emotional and spiritual maturation.

Can astrology predict my career?

Astrology may successfully predict all events related to your life. It serves as an ultimate guiding tool for choosing the right career for you. The choice of career largely depends upon your level and type of education. So, the right career choice starts much before your working age, and astrological guidance must be taken to select the right education stream.

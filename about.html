Authors at Astar

Elizabeth Greeneen

Liz Greene is held in high esteem by astrologers all over the world, professional and non-professional alike. She is a prolific author of books and various publications and has been instrumental in shaping modern psychological astrology.

Bernadette Brady

Bernadette Brady is a professional astrologer, author, astrology instructor (Astrologie-in-Berlin), active and certified member of the Deutscher Astrologen-Verband/DAV. One of Bernadette Brady's specialties is partnership astrology.

Darrelyn Gunzburg

Darrenlyn Gunzburg is one of the world's best-known and most respected astrological authors. She deals mainly with the philosophical and historical dimension of astrology. Gunzburg is an honorary graduate of Brandeis University, graduated in history.

Johannes Schneider

Johannes Schneider is one of the most renowned astrologers in the German-speaking world. She is a graduate psychologist, heads the Training Centre Berlin of the German Astrologers' Association, is the author of many specialist books
